Remote Server Administration

Managing your own servers involves both time and money. Typically you may need a full time staff, constant training for them to keep up with changing technology and often you will focus more time managing your technology than your business. Outsource server management support to us and with our remote server management services we can free you so that you can focus on your core business activities.

Today's server rooms and data centers are challenging environments to control and manage. IT staffs need to be experts in all types of equipment that make up today's data centers, including servers, routers, switches and other devices. They are charged with maintaining operations performance in multiple locations with limited resources - while keeping costs down.

Our Remote Server Management services include 24x7 server monitoring, server health and other well-being check. The checks are followed up with a systems report and suggested courses of action in the event an issue is reported. This service offers company the ultimate peace of mind-knowing IT professionals are "in the loop" about new threats and issues and are proactively keeping a watchful eye over your server(s).

Leverage our expertise to accelerate or expand your company's IT operations. Our engineers are highly qualified with international certifications and our experts will work with your team to understand your needs, plan strategic implementation, and deliver tangible results.

Services Offered

24x7 proactive remote monitoring of connectivity and server services from our central Network Operations Center (NOC)
Monitor and collect data to trend Memory, CPU and Disk utilization. The trends allow proactive suggestions for hardware resource management
Server virtualization and management solutions
24x7 technical support, server log monitoring and proactive resolution
Server security and user access control
Perating system patch updates, anti-virus definition updates, etc
Weekly server maintenance tasks
Managed server backups and restores
Disaster recovery planning and management

Value Proposition

Improved uptime and productivity
Allows more time to concentrate on core business activity
Enhanced ability to adopt or migrate to new technologies
Reduces operational cost thus increasing Return on IT Investment (ROI)
24x7 access to professional and certified domain experts
Implementation of industry best practices
