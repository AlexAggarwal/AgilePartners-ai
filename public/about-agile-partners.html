Agile Partners Canada is a leading and a globally focused ITES (Information Technology Enabled Services) company, providing reliable and affordable Remote Server Administration, Customer Support and Help Desk Services, Web Application Development and Web Site Designing. We specialize in the area of Remote Infrastructure Management (RIMs) and On-Site Support mainly to environments running Microsoft Technologies.

Our technical expertise remains our primary and dominant strength. A dynamic and young team of professionals with strong academic backgrounds, international exposure and advanced technical skills allows us to provide a unique blend of offshore outsourcing experience to our clients.

We are one of the leading services providers having a remarkable track record of consistently delivering workable and robust solutions by adopting innovative approaches and remaining committed to quality, processes and outstanding technological prowess.

Achievements

Complete financial stability since the company's inception and grown solely based on internally generated funds.
Consistently receiving over 85% of business from existing customers. This is because of following a timely delivery record of successful project deliveries since 1997.
Over these years, through technology driven business process transformation initiatives we have mastered the processes, methodologies, approach, mindset and infrastructure needed to overcome the challenges of operating teams which deliver solutions - 24 x 7.
We have always offered major assistance in reducing Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) while increasing the Return on Investment (ROI) for our customers and improving productivity, reliability, quality and speed.
We have maintained a phenomenal growth over all these years.
