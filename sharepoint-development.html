SharePoint Development

Microsoft SharePoint is quickly becoming a critical and versatile platform for collaboration within organizations. With the ease of use and wide range of customization options available, employees within the organization are able to manage projects, collaborate with custom document libraries, create their own sites, and develop dashboards to show MIS reports using Business Intelligence (BI) and Key Performance Indicators (KPI).

Our professionals, trained on Windows SharePoint Services (WSS) and Microsoft Office SharePoint Portal Server (MOSS) can quickly help you to discover how your organization can benefit from implementing SharePoint as part of your Intranet. We can help to provide a detailed services management planning, design the architecture, develop a roadmap to ensure project success, customize and deploy the solution and finally train people to use it for a successful roll-out.

Value Proposition

Tailor-made design and architecture in SharePoint which maps directly to your dynamic business needs.
Affordable business and collaboration solutions for SharePoint including Intranets, vendor collaboration portals, websites and web content management, enterprise search, document management solutions, workflows and form automation, project collaboration and many more.
Tight integration with Microsoft Exchange Server to share task, schedules, meetings and other relevant data with your Outlook Shared Calendar.
Boost employee productivity by simplifying everyday business activities.
Help meet regulatory requirements through comprehensive control over content and content archival.
Effectively manage and repurpose content to gain increased business insights.
Enable people to make better informed decisions by presenting business critical information in one central location without divulging sensitive information.
Reduce development time and costs with our out-of-the-box solutions developed and customized for SharePoint.
